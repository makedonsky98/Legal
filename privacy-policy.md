# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 12, 2026

At **Quantum Apps**, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

---

### 1. Information Collection
We collect information that you provide directly to us through the app's features:
* **Account Credentials:** Email address and name (via Firebase Authentication).
* **Profile Information:** Profile pictures that you choose to upload (stored in Firebase Storage).
* **Usage Data:** Standard logs provided by Firebase Services to ensure app stability.

### 2. Use of Data
Your data is used solely to:
* Provide and maintain the application's core functionality.
* Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
* Allow you to personalize your user profile.

### 3. Third-Party Services
We utilize **Google Firebase** for backend services. These services may collect information used to identify you. You can review their privacy policy at:
* [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* [Firebase Privacy and Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

### 4. Data Deletion (Your Rights)
We believe in full control over your data. 
* You can update your profile information at any time.
* You can **permanently delete your account** and all associated data via the "Delete Account" feature in the app settings.
